🔌 Circuit Descriptions
Control Circuit
24VAC from transformer → thermostat → high limit → gas valve coil. All safety switches in series.
Ignition Circuit
Ignition module (P/N 50239) generates HV spark. Flame sensor monitors µA signal. Module locks out after 3 retries.
Blower Circuit
3-phase or single-phase motor. Air pressure switch proves airflow before gas valve energizes. 2300 RPM nominal.
Conveyor Circuit
Variable speed via VFD or pot-control. Encoder values: ~260 Test / ~30 Normal-Low / ~380 Normal-High (NORA screen).
Safety Circuit
High limit (650°F trips), air pressure switch, door switch, manual reset thermal – all in series, de-energize gas valve.
